About Me

Hey! I'm currently a Computer Science student at Texas A&M University. I am dedicated to sharing my coding knowledge and experience with younger peers. My goal is to create mobile apps that empower and entertain people of all backgrounds. As a first-generation student I plan to return home and share my journey with the youth of my similar background.

Experience

Engineering Peer Teacher

As a Texas A&M Peer Teacher, I dedicate 20 hours on average during the school year to helping 150+ students how to write clean and efficient code in Python from scratch. What I enjoy the most is creating a close relationship with students and learning about all their different backgrounds and experiences.
